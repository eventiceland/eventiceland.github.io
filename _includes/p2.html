Ready To Go

Let us be your guide as we drive you to Iceland's spectacular and hidden wonders in the safety and comfort of our small fleet of 4x4 offroad vehicles. On the way, you will discover lava fields, unique waterfalls, ice lagoons, ice caves, glacier-capped volcanoes, black sand beaches and many more breathtaking locations, each of them a photographer's dream.

Latest Photography Knowledge

Mika Ceron is a fashion photographer, who is shooting campaigns for many international brands. His campaign pictures are published in magazines like Vogue, Elle, Grazia and many more. Mika Ceron will share the secrets of his success as he demonstrates and recreates all kinds of fashion, advertising and landscape photographic set ups, guiding you in your own personal photoshoots. You will also experience and learn some of the latest digital workflow solutions undertaking some shoots while directly tethered to a workstation. Additionally, you will receive valuable knowledge and guidance on how to work on set with studio flash generators and how to achieve special lighting effects like the well known American Night. Best of all, you will be shooting your own sensational photographs throughout.

Phase One

The students of the workshop can try during the workshop one of the best digital medium format systems of Phase One. With 60 Megapixel in high end quality and with the unique Capture One workflow solution. Mika Ceron will explain the possibilities of leaf shutter lenses combined with flash lights used outdoor and many other features of this system.
